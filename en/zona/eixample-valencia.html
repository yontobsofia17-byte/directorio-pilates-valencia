Pilates in the Eixample, Valencia: everything you need to know

The Eixample and Ensanche of Valencia have the greatest density of pilates studios in the city. You'll find boutique reformer-only studios as well as centres combining pilates with clinical physiotherapy. It is Valencia's reference area for high-quality pilates.

Comparing studios before you decide is key: look at equipment type (reformer only, mat, or all machines), whether they offer clinical physiotherapy, available schedules and whether classes are taught in English. Filter by what matters most to you in the full directory.

Frequently asked questions about pilates in the Eixample, Valencia

How many pilates studios are there in the Eixample?

The directory lists over 10 pilates studios in the Eixample and Ensanche of Valencia, including boutique reformer centres, studios with physiotherapy and full-machine centres.

How much does pilates cost in the Eixample?

Studios in the Eixample and Ensanche have varied prices: from group classes at €15–20 to private sessions above €40. Monthly memberships range from €90 to €155 for 2 sessions per week.

Is there pilates with physiotherapy in the Eixample?

Yes, several studios in the Eixample and Ensanche combine pilates with clinical physiotherapy, ideal for rehabilitation or supervised therapeutic pilates.
