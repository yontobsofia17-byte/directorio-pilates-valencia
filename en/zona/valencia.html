Pilates in Valencia: everything you need to know

Valencia city has the largest pilates offering in the Valencian Community. You'll find boutique reformer-only studios, full-machine centres and spaces combining pilates with physiotherapy. The neighbourhoods with the most choice are Eixample, Ruzafa, Extramurs and Ciutat Vella.

Comparing studios before you decide is key: look at equipment type (reformer only, mat, or all machines), whether they offer clinical physiotherapy, available schedules and whether classes are taught in English. Filter by what matters most to you in the full directory.

Frequently asked questions about pilates in Valencia

How many pilates studios are there in Valencia city?

The directory lists over 30 pilates studios in Valencia city, spread across neighbourhoods including Eixample, Ensanche, Ruzafa, Ciutat Vella, Patraix and Extramurs.

Which Valencia neighbourhoods have the most pilates studios?

The Eixample-Ensanche area has the highest concentration of boutique studios, followed by Ruzafa, Ciutat Vella and Patraix. There are also centres in El Cabanyal, Extramurs, Algirós and Quatre Carreres.

How much does pilates cost in Valencia city?

Drop-in classes range from €10 to €49 per session, and monthly memberships for 2 sessions per week between €78 and €155 depending on the studio and format (reformer or mat).

Is there reformer pilates in Valencia?

Yes, the majority of studios in the directory offer reformer or full-machine pilates, especially in the Eixample, Extramurs and Ensanche areas.
