How to choose your pilates modality

The choice depends on three factors: your goal (tone up, rehabilitate, general wellbeing), your budget and the format you prefer (individual or group class). If you have an injury or specific condition, pilates with physiotherapy is the safest option. If you're starting out, mat or reformer are the best entry points. For a more complete experience of the method, studios with all the machines offer the greatest variety.
