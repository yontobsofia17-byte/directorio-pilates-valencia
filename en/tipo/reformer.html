What is reformer pilates?

Reformer pilates is the most sought-after pilates format in Valencia. Unlike mat pilates, the reformer machine uses springs and pulleys to work resistance, control and alignment. It is recommended for rehabilitation, pregnancy, athletes and people with injuries. Prices range from drop-in sessions to monthly memberships, with groups of 1–6 people depending on the studio.

Frequently asked questions

What is reformer pilates?

Reformer pilates uses a spring-and-pulley machine that adds resistance or assistance to movements, allowing more precise and adaptable work than mat pilates. It is especially effective for rehabilitation, postural control and progressive strengthening.

Who is reformer pilates for?

Reformer is suitable for any level, from beginners to athletes. It is especially recommended for people in rehabilitation, pregnant women, those with back or knee injuries, and anyone looking for a more technical, personalised practice.

How much does reformer pilates cost in Valencia?

Drop-in reformer sessions range from €17 to €49. Monthly memberships for 2 sessions per week are between €95 and €155 depending on the studio. Reformer typically costs 30–60 % more than mat pilates due to the equipment and smaller class sizes.

How many people are in a reformer pilates class?

Reformer groups are usually 2–6 people, much smaller than mat pilates classes, which ensures more attention from the instructor and a more personalised practice.
