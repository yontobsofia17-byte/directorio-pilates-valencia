Pilates with Physiotherapy in Valencia

Pilates studios in Valencia offering clinical physiotherapy services. Ideal for rehabilitation, injuries or supervised therapeutic pilates.

Pilates with physiotherapy centres combine clinical pilates with the assessment and follow-up of qualified physiotherapists. Before starting, you receive an individual assessment to identify limitations, injuries or specific needs.

Exercises are adapted to your condition and supervised throughout your progression. It's especially indicated for rehabilitation, chronic pain, pelvic floor dysfunction, post-operative recovery and neurological conditions.

Ideal for: People with injuries, chronic pain, post-operative recovery, pelvic floor issues, neurological conditions, or anyone wanting clinical oversight of their Pilates practice.

Benefits

  • Initial clinical assessment by a physiotherapist
  • Exercises adapted to your specific condition
  • Safe for rehabilitation and post-operative recovery
  • Specialised pelvic floor and women's health work
  • Professional supervision throughout progression
  • Combines therapeutic benefit with physical training

Frequently asked questions

What is the advantage of pilates with physiotherapy?

Combining pilates with physiotherapy allows a prior clinical assessment, adaptation of exercises to specific injuries or conditions, and follow-up by a qualified physiotherapist. It is especially useful for rehabilitation, chronic pain and pelvic floor work.

Is pilates with clinical physiotherapy more expensive?

Generally yes, centres with physiotherapists on staff have slightly higher rates than conventional studios, especially for therapeutic or rehabilitation pilates. The level of personalisation and safety is higher.

Is therapeutic pilates only for people with injuries?

No, it is also recommended as prevention for sedentary people, athletes with muscular imbalances, older adults, and pregnant or postnatal women who want more clinical oversight of their practice.
